Blooming Beads

Stay connected by learning about your loved ones and watch as your relationships and mutual knowing bloom!

Game Objectives

  • Leverage play as a modality for learning and social connection
  • Improve motor coordination through embodied movements
  • Practice executive functions & cognitive skills
  • Foster intergenerational collaboration & interaction through sharing of knowledge and experiences

Birds of a feather...

Blooming Beads is a collaborative game that recognizes that supporting one another is always beneficial! Players must save one another to remain connected throughout the game. This motivates individual players to help each other for the betterment of the entire team. It is not about individual success but rather highlights the important role you play within a team.. Schools often face resource limitations and the nuanced concepts of core STEM concepts like programming and computational thinking makes them challenging to teach at an early age.
