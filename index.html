Byte Beads

Byte Beads is a game designed help children develop computational thinking and executive function skills. By making STEM education more accessible, the game fosters active collaboration and engagement through sound sequence creation.

  • Learn More
Beads of a feather string together!

Blooming Beads

Blooming Beads is an intergenerational tabletop game for children and older adults alike that playfully integrates sound, body movement, and storytelling to provide family and friends with a joyful experience, fostering deeper human connection and promoting health and well-being.
